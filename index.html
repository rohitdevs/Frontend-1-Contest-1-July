Water activities in Goa

  1. Jet Skiing: Experience the thrill of riding a powerful water scooter as you zoom across the waves.
  2. Parasailing: Get a bird's-eye view of the stunning coastline while being harnessed to a parachute and towed by a speedboat.
  3. Banana Boat Ride: Hop on an inflatable banana-shaped boat and hold on tight as it's pulled by a speedboat, bouncing over the waves.
  4. Scuba Diving: Explore the vibrant underwater world, discovering coral reefs, marine life, and shipwrecks with the help of professional dive instructors.
  5. Snorkeling: Discover the underwater beauty of Goa by swimming near the surface with a snorkel, mask, and fins, observing the colorful marine life.
  6. Kayaking: Paddle through the serene backwaters, mangroves, and estuaries, immersing yourself in the picturesque landscapes.

Things to bring

  • Swimwear: Pack your swimsuit or trunks to wear during water activities. Choose comfortable, quick-drying materials.
  • Sunscreen: Carry a waterproof sunscreen with a high SPF to protect your skin from sunburn. Apply it generously and reapply as needed, especially if you'll be in the water for extended periods.
  • Towel: Carry a lightweight and quick-drying towel to dry off after water activities.
  • Change of Clothes: Bring a change of clothes for after the water activities, so you can stay comfortable during your journey back.
